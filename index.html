Confidence grades

Each grade reflects the worst-case 95% Wilson confidence interval margin across all drops for that mob. A narrow interval means the displayed drop rate is close to the true rate; a wide one means more kills are needed.


A < ±2% margin — very reliable (>1000 kills typically)
B ±2–5% — reliable (300–1000 kills)
C ±5–10% — decent (80–300 kills)
D ±10–15% — sparse (30–80 kills)
F > ±15% — too few kills to trust (<30)

⚠️ Rates may be slightly inflated — kills from other players in render distance can be counted. High-confidence grades reduce but don't eliminate this bias.
